Girls Build With Code

A computer science organization that empowers girls and young women to build great things with code.

About GBWC:

Girls Build With Code aims to encourage, support, and increase the participation of women in computer science.
Through workshops, projects, and networking, we are able to reach and motivate female students to build amazing projects
with code!

 

Why is this important?

Despite women making there way into the tech field, oftentimes, many may feel demotivated to continue in their career paths due to various factors, oftentimes irrelevant to their capabilities. At GBWC, we work to encourage girls to continue pursuing computer science through exposure, by builiding impactful projects.
